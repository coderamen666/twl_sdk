MB_COMM_PARENT_*

Definition

#include <nitro/mb.h>

#define MB_COMM_PARENT_SEND_MIN   256
#define MB_COMM_PARENT_SEND_MAX   510

#define MB_COMM_PARENT_RECV_MIN   8
#define MB_COMM_PARENT_RECV_MAX   16

Description

Constant showing available range for wireless communication data size setting of multiboot parent. For details, refer to the MB_SetParentCommSize and MB_SetParentCommParam functions.

MB_SetParentCommSize, MB_SetParentCommParam
