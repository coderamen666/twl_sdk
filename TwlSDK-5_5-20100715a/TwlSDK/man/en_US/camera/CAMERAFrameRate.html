CAMERAFrameRate

Definition

#include <twl/camera.h>


typedef enum
{
    CAMERA_FRAME_RATE_15,
    CAMERA_FRAME_RATE_15_TO_5,
    CAMERA_FRAME_RATE_15_TO_2,
    CAMERA_FRAME_RATE_8_5,
    CAMERA_FRAME_RATE_5,
    CAMERA_FRAME_RATE_20,
    CAMERA_FRAME_RATE_20_TO_5,
    CAMERA_FRAME_RATE_30,
    CAMERA_FRAME_RATE_30_TO_5,
    CAMERA_FRAME_RATE_15_TO_10,
    CAMERA_FRAME_RATE_20_TO_10,
    CAMERA_FRAME_RATE_30_TO_10,

    CAMERA_FRAME_RATE_MAX
}
CAMERAFrameRate;

Elements

CAMERA_FRAME_RATE_15 Fixed at 15 fps.
CAMERA_FRAME_RATE_15_TO_5 Changes automatically between 15 and 5 fps, depending on the brightness.
CAMERA_FRAME_RATE_15_TO_2 Changes automatically between 15 and 2 fps, depending on the brightness.
CAMERA_FRAME_RATE_8_5 Fixed at 8.5 fps.
CAMERA_FRAME_RATE_5 Fixed at 5 fps.
CAMERA_FRAME_RATE_20 Fixed at 20 fps.
CAMERA_FRAME_RATE_20_TO_5 Changes automatically between 20 and 5 fps, depending on the brightness.
CAMERA_FRAME_RATE_30 Fixed at 30 fps.
CAMERA_FRAME_RATE_30_TO_5 Changes automatically between 30 and 5 fps, depending on the brightness.
CAMERA_FRAME_RATE_15_TO_10 Changes automatically between 15 and 10 fps, depending on the brightness.
CAMERA_FRAME_RATE_20_TO_10 Changes automatically between 20 and 10 fps, depending on the brightness.
CAMERA_FRAME_RATE_30_TO_10 Changes automatically between 30 and 10 fps, depending on the brightness.

Description

This is an enumerated type that specifies the camera's frame rate.
